Here is a brief description of some of the most popular anime movie that have won many awards and fans :

"Spirited Away" (Sen to Chihiro no Kamikakushi) : Composed by renowned director Hayao Miyazaki, this film follows the journey of Chihiro, a girl who is lost in the spirit world and must work hard to save her parents. "Spirited Away" is recognized as one of the greatest anime films of all time.
"Your Name" (Kimi no Na wa) : A story about a magical body swap between two teenagers, Taki and Mitsuha, who live in different places. This film combines elements of romance and science fiction beautifully and deeply.
"Grave of the Fireflies" (Hotaru no Haka) : This film tells the sad, emotional story of two orphans struggling to survive in post-World War II Japan. This is a very emotional and soulful film.
"A Silent Voice" (Koe no Katachi) : This film explores themes such as sensitivity, repentance, and repentance. It follows the story of a young man who tries to atone for his past mistakes.
The anime movie above are examples of the various genres, styles and stories that you can find in the world of anime. They have wide appeal and have made a huge impact on the world of global entertainment.
